About Us

About Us
Camp Pontiac is located in the foothills of the Berkshires just two hours from New York City. Camp Pontiac is a 7 week sleep away camp that has been around since 1922. Camp Pontiac is a co-ed camp with both a girls' side and boys' side located on the property.
Activities
We specialize in activities ranging from cooking, outdoor adventure, waterfront activities, sports, arts and crafts, and so much more. At Camp Pontiac, there is an activity for everyone. Contact us to learn more about privates and specialized instruction!
