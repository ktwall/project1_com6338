About Camp Pontiac:

Camp Pontiac is one of the leading sleepaway camps in the state of New York. Located less than two hours from NYC, we are located in Copake, New York. Camp Pontiac is open to girls and boys aged 7 to 16. Every summer, our campers join us for 7 weeks starting in late June and staying until early August. Please read the summer dates section for exacts dates for the upcoming summer. Camp Pontiac has been serving boys and girls since 1922. Please click on the About section to learn more about a summer at Camp Pontiac.
